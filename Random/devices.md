# Devices needed

* 1x Switch
* 2x PC
* 5x Raspberry Pi
* 12x USB Network Adapters
* 15x CAT6- cables
* Devices for testing

## Notes

* Switch devices should be much powerfull than Raspberry Pi's
* Internal network adapters are recommendable
* Firewall PC should be powerfull (at least i7) and have three internal NIC's
